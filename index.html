biography

A polymer & material science engineer by education, years after a successful engineering career, life took a turn and with some soul searching, here I am, doing what I have always wanted to do. Driven by music through all the stages of my life, music mixing and production for me is far beyond the lyrics, beats or anything that words could describe.

“If you can close your eyes and my music makes you feel something deeper within your soul, I’ve done what I wanted to do.”


Love,
Rewyre
